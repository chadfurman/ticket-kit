# TD-0005 · Show subtasks on the detail page

## Why

When you open a parent ticket you want its subtasks right there, and a subtask
should link back up to its parent.

## What

- A relations strip above the prose: `↑ parent: …` and a `subtasks:` list.
- Done subtasks render with a check and a strikethrough.

## Acceptance

- [ ] Parent link renders on a subtask's detail page.
- [ ] Subtask list renders on a parent's detail page.
